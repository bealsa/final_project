About Me

I am a second-year Master of Health Informatics student in the School of Information at the University of Michigan. I received my BS from Eastern Michigan University in Public Health Education

I have an interest in research that uses data science to explore health disparities. I am also interested in data visualization and graphic design. Currently, I am involved in research that explores health behavior activity on social media and taking course work in graphic design and website design.

For future research and career, I would like to utilize the tools I have learned from my current program to uncover health disparities occurring in communities and find ways to promote policies that will address them.
